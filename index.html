Privacy Policy

Thank you for using BurnIt.

BurnIt is an AI-powered application that helps users estimate the approximate calorie content of food from photos and understand the estimated amount of physical activity required to burn those calories.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have when using BurnIt.

Information We Collect

  • Food photos that you voluntarily submit for calorie estimation.
  • Manual food entries and calorie information that you choose to enter.
  • Your weight, preferred activity, daily calorie goal, and other app settings.
  • Food history and analysis results stored locally on your device.
  • Anonymous device and usage information used to enforce daily scan limits and improve app reliability.
  • Subscription status information used to determine whether you have access to BurnIt Pro.

BurnIt does not require users to create an account to use the app.

How We Use Information

  • To analyze food photos and estimate calories, macronutrients, and portion information.
  • To calculate estimated activity time required to burn calories.
  • To save your app settings and food history on your device.
  • To verify subscription status and provide Pro features.
  • To enforce free daily usage limits.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

BurnIt does not sell your personal information.

AI Image Processing

When you scan or upload a food photo, the image is securely transmitted for processing by trusted AI service providers solely for the purpose of food recognition and calorie estimation.

Uploaded food images are used only to provide the analysis requested by the user and are not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

BurnIt does not intentionally retain uploaded food images longer than necessary to provide the requested analysis. Analysis results and food history may be stored locally on your device.

Subscriptions and Payments

BurnIt Pro subscriptions are processed through Apple App Store In-App Purchases. BurnIt does not collect, store, or process your payment card information.

BurnIt may use RevenueCat to verify subscription status and determine whether Pro features are available.

Third-Party Services

BurnIt may use trusted third-party services to operate the application, including:

  • AI service providers for food image analysis.
  • RevenueCat for subscription management.
  • Apple App Store for in-app purchase processing.

These providers process information only as necessary to perform their services and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Most user settings and food history are stored locally on your device. If the application is removed or reinstalled, locally stored information, including food history images, may no longer be available.

BurnIt may store limited anonymous usage data, such as scan counts and subscription status, to help maintain service reliability and prevent abuse.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Children's Privacy

BurnIt is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Health Disclaimer

BurnIt provides estimated calorie and nutrition information for general informational purposes only. Results may be inaccurate or incomplete and should not be considered medical, dietary, nutritional, or professional advice.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making decisions related to your diet, health, weight, or exercise.

Your Choices

You may delete the application at any time to remove locally stored data from your device. You may also contact us regarding privacy-related questions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
