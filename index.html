Privacy Policy

Thank you for using BurnIt.

BurnIt is an AI-powered application that helps users estimate the approximate calorie content of food from photos and understand the estimated amount of physical activity required to burn those calories.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have when using BurnIt.

Information We Collect

  • Food photos that you voluntarily submit for calorie estimation.
  • Manual food entries and calorie information that you choose to enter.
  • Your weight, preferred activity, daily calorie goal, and other app settings.
  • Food history and analysis results saved locally on your device.
  • Anonymous device and usage information used to enforce daily scan limits and improve app reliability.
  • Subscription status information used to determine whether you have access to BurnIt Pro.

BurnIt does not require users to create an account to use the app.

How We Use Information

  • To analyze food photos and estimate calories, macronutrients, and portion information.
  • To calculate estimated activity time required to burn calories.
  • To save your app settings and food history on your device.
  • To verify subscription status and provide Pro features.
  • To enforce free daily usage limits.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

BurnIt does not sell your personal information.

AI Image Processing

When you scan or upload a food photo, the image is securely sent to BurnIt's server and then processed by trusted AI services for the purpose of food recognition and calorie estimation.

Food images are used only to provide the analysis requested by the user. BurnIt does not use uploaded food photos for advertising or marketing purposes.

Food photos are not intended to be permanently stored by BurnIt. Analysis results and food history may be stored locally on your device.

Subscriptions and Payments

BurnIt Pro subscriptions are processed through Apple App Store in-app purchases. BurnIt does not collect, store, or process your payment card information.

BurnIt may use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and determine whether Pro features are available.

Third-Party Services

BurnIt may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • OpenAI for AI-powered food image analysis.
  • RevenueCat for subscription management.
  • Apple App Store for in-app purchase processing.

These services process information only as necessary to provide their respective functionality and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Most user settings and food history are stored locally on your device. If the app is deleted or reinstalled, locally stored data, including images displayed in history, may be lost.

BurnIt may store limited anonymous usage data on its server, such as scan counts and usage limits, to keep the service reliable and prevent abuse.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

BurnIt is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Health and Nutrition Disclaimer

BurnIt provides estimated calorie and nutrition information for general informational purposes only. Results may be inaccurate or incomplete and should not be considered medical, dietary, nutritional, or professional advice.

You should consult a qualified healthcare or nutrition professional before making decisions related to your health, diet, weight loss, medical conditions, or exercise routine.

Your Choices

You may delete the app at any time to remove locally stored app data from your device. You may also contact us to request assistance with privacy-related questions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
